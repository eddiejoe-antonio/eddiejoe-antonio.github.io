Marsha P. Johnson State Park

Over a 3 month period, I participated in a series of both virtual and in-person outreach sessions guiding the renovation of Marsha P. Johnson State Park. I facilitated breakout groups, conducted Spanish-language interviews with park users, and helped develop illustrative, planting, and grading plans. I modeled the park redesign through multiple iterations and created perspectival and aerial renderings for public comment, both at outreach meetings and on the NYS Parks website.
