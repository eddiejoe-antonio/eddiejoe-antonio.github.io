Designing A Marine Cemetery in Miami Bay

Sited in Floridaâ€™s Biscayne Bay, this academic project proposed an aquatic cemetery in response to the rich cultural and urban landscape of Miami. As a form of marine urbanism, the city of the dead draws on the specific practices of Miami to produce spaces that facilitate funerary ritual.

The neighborhood parade, the spiritual procession, the community dance, the somber, family oriented burial, and the days of worship and return are here given priority and offered prominent roles in the burial system through an exchange between family crypts and neighborhood plazas. The procession descends into the dark, privacy of the subterranean, introducing a new relationship to Biscayne Bay while providing a comfortable, shaded space for mourning.
