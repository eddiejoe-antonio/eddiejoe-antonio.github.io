Temple Hoyne Buell Center at Columbia University: Morningside

As part of a larger initiative to interrogate land in the Americas, I have developed historical sections of the site of the Buell Center. Illustrating conditions back to the early 1600s, the sections are detailed conceptual drawings that highlight the constraining, leveling, and manipulating of land in the heart of Morningside Heights.
