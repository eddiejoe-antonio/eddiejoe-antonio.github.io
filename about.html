Eddie Joe Antonio is a computational designer, researcher, and digital equity analyst. He is a native New Yorker fascinated by cities and how we live in them: spatially, experientially, and digitally. Eddie Joe recently completed a Master of Science in Computational Design Practices (M.S.CDP) at Columbia University where he trained in web development, mapmaking, and computationally-driven analysis.

He's worked as a furniture designer at Gloria Cortina Studio in Mexico City, a landscape designer at Starr Whitehouse Landscape Architects and Planners in New York City, and as a research assistant at the Buell Center at Columbia University. He currently works as an analyst at HR&A in the firm's Broadband & Digital Equity practice.

He loves biking, cooking, gardening, and playing music.
